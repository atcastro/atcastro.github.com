Becoming You

A Short Story of Self-Acceptance


Choose something you like about yourself

Pick something you would like to imporve about yourself

How would you describe the world?

Sometimes it can be easier to see the beauty in others...


Than it is seeing the beauty in yourself.


It can be easier to focus on what makes you different...


Without seeing why it makes you special.

Sometimes...


The world just seems too important.


Loving yourself is one of the hardest challenges you can face in life.


To accept yourself for your flaws isn’t something someone can teach you, but it is a journey we must all take.


But you don’t have to do it alone.


When the world becomes too big, and you feel too small. Taught to someone you trust, or speak with a professional.


Believe the people you love when they tell you how you are.


And know that it’s true.


If you think you are , be patient with yourself.


Think if you would be this hard on someone you care about.


And if you think the world is , know that everyone else does too.


You are not alone. Everyone goes through what you are going through.


Take the good, take the bad, take everything that makes you YOU.


And once you know who you are.


You’ll truly be unstoppable.
